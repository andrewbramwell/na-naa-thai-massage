Intensive & Restorative

Deep Tissue Massage

Experience the transformative power of deep tissue massage at Na Naa Thai Massage. This intensive technique targets chronic muscle tension and knots, providing relief and restoring mobility.

Our expert therapists use slow, deliberate strokes and firm pressure to reach deeper layers of muscle and fascia. We focus on problem areas, working through adhesions and scar tissue to alleviate persistent pain and stiffness. Unlike the gentler Swedish massage, deep tissue work is more intense and may cause slight discomfort, but the results are long-lasting. It's ideal for those with specific muscular issues, athletes, or anyone seeking to address deep-seated tension.

Energizing & Holistic

Traditional Thai Massage

Immerse yourself in the ancient healing art of traditional Thai massage at Na Naa Thai Massage. This unique technique, also known as "Thai yoga massage," combines acupressure, energy work, and assisted yoga postures to restore balance and vitality.

Our skilled practitioners use their hands, knees, legs, and feet to move you into a series of yoga-like stretches. Along with gentle pressure along energy lines, rhythmic compressions, and artful stretching, this massage style works to improve flexibility, reduce tension, and energise both body and mind. Unlike oil-based massages, Thai massage is performed on a padded mat with you fully clothed, allowing for a wide range of motion. It's an invigorating experience that leaves you feeling both relaxed and energised.

Soothing & Circulatory

Swedish Massage

Indulge in the gentle art of Swedish massage at Na Naa Thai Massage. This classic technique aims to enhance circulation and foster deep relaxation by soothing your muscles.

Our skilled therapists employ a variety of soft, flowing strokes tailored to your body's needs. We use light, gliding touches over sensitive areas, deeper and more vigorous kneading on muscular regions, as well as rhythmic tapping and gentle stretching. Unlike deep tissue or sports massages, Swedish massage focuses on overall relaxation and stress relief. It's perfect for those seeking a gentler, more soothing experience.

Rejuvenating & Reflexive

Foot Massage

Treat your feet to blissful relaxation with a rejuvenating foot massage at Na Naa Thai Massage. This targeted therapy focuses on the intricate network of nerves and pressure points in your feet, offering benefits that radiate throughout your entire body.

Our experienced therapists use a combination of techniques including kneading, circular pressures, and precise thumb and finger work to stimulate specific reflex areas. This not only relieves tension and promotes relaxation in your feet and lower legs but also encourages overall well-being. Unlike full-body massages, this concentrated treatment allows for a deeply soothing experience in a shorter time frame. It's perfect for those looking to unwind after a long day on their feet or anyone seeking to improve circulation and reduce stress.
