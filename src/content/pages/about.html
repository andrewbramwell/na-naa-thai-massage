About Na Naa Thai Massage

Na Naa, the founder of Na Naa Thai Massage, brings a wealth of experience and cultural authenticity to her practice. Originally from Thailand, she has called the UK home since 1997, blending Eastern traditions with Western techniques to offer a unique massage experience.

Na Naa's journey in massage therapy began with her own transformative experience. After recovering from an injury through the power of therapeutic massage, she was inspired to share this healing art with others. This personal connection to the practice drives her passion and dedication to helping clients achieve wellness and relief.

With a strong foundation in traditional Thai massage from the prestigious Wat Pho in Thailand, complemented by recognised UK qualifications, Na Naa opened her Kilburn studio in 2018. Since then, she has been committed to providing high-quality, personalised massage treatments that cater to the diverse needs of her clients, witnessing countless transformations and success stories along the way.
